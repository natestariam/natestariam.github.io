01 — The shift

For 20 years, the e-commerce playbook was simple: rank on Google, get the click, convert the visit. Every brand optimised for the same linear funnel. But that funnel assumed a world where people search. Increasingly, they ask.

02 — The new model

Not a funnel. A conversation loop.

The linear funnel assumed drop-off at every stage. The new model is a continuous conversation — between the customer, the AI, and On — that loops through discovery, fit, purchase, and return.

STAGE 01 Be Found
Replaces: SEO + paid search

The AI needs to know On exists AND recommend it. The "digital shelf" is now inside the AI's knowledge base.

  • Rich, structured product data that AI can reason about — not just index. Specs, comparisons, use-case mappings.
  • Authoritative content that trains models to associate On with quality: expert reviews, biomechanics studies, athlete testimonials.
  • Partnerships with AI platforms — ChatGPT plugins, Perplexity Shopping integrations, Google AI Overviews.
  • Being present in AI training data through genuine editorial, not keyword-stuffed SEO copy.
STAGE 02 The Conversation
Replaces: the landing page

When someone arrives via AI recommendation, the experience should continue the conversation — not reset it with a generic homepage.

  • An On AI assistant that picks up where ChatGPT left off: "You were looking for a marathon shoe — here's what we'd recommend."
  • Seamless handoff from AI discovery to On's own conversational commerce experience.
  • No more homepage → category → product → cart. The landing "page" becomes a landing "conversation."

STAGE 03 The Fit
Replaces: browsing the catalogue

AI-powered product matching replaces aimless browsing. The product finds the person.

  • Phone camera foot scan → instant size and width recommendation.
  • "Tell me how you run" conversational quiz → AI matches the right model.
  • Visual search: photo of current worn shoes → AI recommends the On replacement.
  • Returning customers: the digital twin already knows their gait, mileage, preferences.
STAGE 04 The Moment
Replaces: checkout

Frictionless, context-aware conversion. The checkout should feel like a confirmation, not a commitment.

  • One-tap purchase from the AI recommendation — no cart page, no upsell gauntlet.
  • "Your AI recommended these — here's why they're right for you" — a personalised confirmation, not a generic receipt.
  • Smart bundling: shoe + insole + training plan, suggested contextually based on what the conversation revealed.
STAGE 05 The Return
Replaces: abandoned cart emails + promo codes

The relationship IS the retention mechanism. Not a discount. Not a drip campaign.

  • Didn't buy? The On assistant remembers the conversation and follows up intelligently — not with spam.
  • Post-purchase: the digital twin starts learning from their runs (insole data, watch integration).
  • Proactive outreach when the shoe is nearing end-of-life, based on real usage data.
  • AI-generated personalised content: weekly running summary, next-pair recommendation, training insights.
  • The twin becomes the reason they return — not a promo code.
03 — What to build

Four products for the AI-native era

Not roadmap items. Not features. Four distinct products that together form a new commerce architecture — one designed for conversations, not clicks.

PRODUCT 01
On AI Assistant
A conversational shopping companion on on.com. Not a chatbot reading FAQs — a knowledgeable advisor that understands the full product range, can discuss fit, terrain, running style, and picks up where ChatGPT or Perplexity left off. The new front door.
PRODUCT 02
AEO Programme
A dedicated AI Engine Optimisation effort. Structured product data, expert content strategy, review corpus management, and direct partnerships with AI platforms. The goal: when any AI recommends running shoes, On is in the answer.
PRODUCT 03
The Proactive Twin
Post-purchase AI that doesn't wait for the customer to return. Using insole and watch data, it predicts when the next pair is needed, suggests the right model based on evolving running patterns, and initiates the conversation at the right moment.
PRODUCT 04
AI-Native Storefront
A version of on.com designed not for human browsers but for AI agents. Structured, semantic, API-first. When someone's AI shopping agent queries "best trail shoe under $200", this storefront serves the answer directly.
